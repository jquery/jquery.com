We used to feature a site for organizing jQuery meetups. Since that didn't really go anywhere, we've shut it down and replaced it with this page.

If you're looking for meetups, check out jquery.meetup.com

Meetup-in-a-bag

If you're organizing a jQuery or JavaScript meetup and want to help the jQuery Foundation with recruiting individual members, we can help you with a meetup-in-a-bag. This consists of:

  • A large commuter bag
  • Five t-shirts in various sizes
  • Lots of stickers
  • Coupons
  • iPhone skins

We just need your address and something to show that you're serious about organizing a meetup. You can contact us by email.
